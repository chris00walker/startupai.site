### Testable Hypotheses + Fast Experiments

#### Hypothesis 1: White-Label is Mandatory, Not Optional

**Statement**: "Consultants will not adopt if deliverables are branded 'Powered by StartupAI.' White-label capability is table stakes, not a feature upgrade."

**Test**: Beta recruitment messaging A/B

- Variant A: "White-label MVP delivery for your clients" (lead with this benefit)
- Variant B: "AI-powered MVP platform for consultants" (no white-label mention)
- Success Criteria: Variant A has >3x sign-up rate = white-label is critical

**Measurement**: Sign-up rate, qualification call questions ("How does white-label work?")

---

#### Hypothesis 2: Margin Matters More Than Absolute Cost

**Statement**: "Consultants evaluate $499/mo based on 'Can I bill $2-4K per project?' (margin focus) not 'Is $499 a lot?' (absolute cost focus). Price objections are actually margin objections."

**Test**: Pricing page split test

- Variant A: "$499/seat per month"
- Variant B: "$499/seat per month â†’ Bill clients $3-7K per MVP (2-4x margin)"
- Success Criteria: Variant B has >40% higher conversion (from pricing page to demo call)

**Measurement**: Conversion rate, demo call attendance

---

#### Hypothesis 3: Case Studies > Feature Lists for Consultant Audience

**Statement**: "Consultants need proof from peers ('Has another consultant done this successfully?') more than feature specs. Social proof is highest driver."

**Test**: Landing page above-fold content

- Variant A: Feature list (white-label, client workspaces, quality, speed)
- Variant B: Case study headline: "'I added $80K in MVP revenue without hiring' - Consultant X"
- Success Criteria: Variant B has >30% higher scroll depth + >20% more demo requests

**Measurement**: Scroll depth, time on page, demo request rate

---

#### Hypothesis 4: Revenue Growth > Time Savings as Primary Driver

**Statement**: "Consultants are more motivated by 'Add $50-150K/yr revenue' than 'Save 30 hours per client.' Revenue expansion beats efficiency."

**Test**: Email nurture sequence A/B

- Variant A: Emphasize time savings ("Deliver MVPs in 7 days vs. 3 months")
- Variant B: Emphasize revenue growth ("Turn $5K strategy engagements into $12K full-stack projects")
- Success Criteria: Variant B has >25% higher click-through rate to demo scheduler

**Measurement**: Email CTR, demo booking rate

---

#### Hypothesis 5: Client-Facing Quality > Behind-the-Scenes Process

**Statement**: "Consultants care more about 'How will this look to my client?' than 'How does the AI work?' Client perception is the priority."

**Test**: Demo call structure A/B

- Variant A: Show technical process (how AI orchestrates tools, code quality metrics)
- Variant B: Show client-facing deliverables (MVP demo, business model canvas, client presentation deck)
- Success Criteria: Variant B leads to >40% higher conversion to pilot program enrollment

**Measurement**: Demo-to-pilot conversion rate, post-demo survey feedback
